Vice President's Branch Overview

The Vice President's Branch is composed of the Vice President's Staff and Programs

The Vice President's Branch is mainly over activities regarding the college campus and its affiliates.

Vice President's Staff covers the people and respective divisions that the Vice President has appointed with the approval of the student senate. One example is Communications team, which is responsible for controlling the SGA social media pages and taking photos at events.

Programs is in charge of the school outreach that was listed above. Student government tabling and collaboration events with places like the Health Center and Provost Office are a few notible examples of their roles.
