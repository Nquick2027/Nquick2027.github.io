Treasurer's Branch Overview

The Treasurer's Branch is composed of the Treasurer's Staff, Comptrollers, and Finance Committee.

The Treasurer's Branch oversees all funding operations of the SGA and school clubs that request for it.

Treasurer's Staff are those appointed by the Treasurer for specific roles.

Comptrollers are charged with working with a select number of student clubs and organizations to provided or reject fundig for their events. They also have the responsibility of running the Treasurer's workshops to teach clubs how to request and manage funding.

This branch also has joint ownership over the Finance Commitee which controls the budget of Student Goverment as well as overall club funding. They are the ones who can reject funding or put funding requests up to a vote within the Student Senate regarding difficult requests.
