President's Branch Overview

The president's branch is composed of the Executive Cabinet, Freshman Council, and President's Staff.

The focus on the more political outreach aspects of student government, like communicating with the USC President and Board of Trustees

I do not know the functions of the Executive Cabinet but I do know one of them has the roll of elections commisioner, overseeing officer elections for the four major rolls when the time comes.

Freshman Council is the way many members of student government start out. They act as freshman student representatives in the student senate. After their first year, they may branch out into the other branches or be appointed as formal senators for an elected college.

The President's Staff are the people the president directly appoints to lead divisions and innititives he has created.
